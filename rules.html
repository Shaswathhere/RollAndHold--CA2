Rules

  1. 1) This game consist of 2 players . Player takes turn to roll the dice . On their turn they can roll the die as many times they want in a single turn .

  2. 2) Once the player decides to hold , the turn for the player is over and the turn’s points will become the overall score . Then the other player continues the same .

  3. 3) When the player rolls the dice and if the number is even their current score will become 0 and they will lose their turn .

  4. 4) Before the start of the game players can set the maximum score and the first player to obtain the maximum score wins
